## Context
This project involved a critical evaluation of a live Webflow site to uncover friction points impacting the user experience. My goal was to identify design inconsistencies and usability flaws, and then present clear, data-informed solutions to the client to guide their future development efforts.

## Goals
The primary goal was to conduct a thorough analysis of the client's Webflow website to identify critical design and usability issues. The key objectives were:
Conduct a heuristic evaluation, Assess UI consistency and accessibility, and deliver actionable recommendations.

## Process
My approach was methodical and structured to ensure that all findings were based on established UX principles and directly addressed the client's business goals.

## Outcome
The client received a detailed UX audit report that identified over 25 distinct usability issues, prioritized by severity and potential impact. The report served as a strategic roadmap, empowering their team with a clear understanding of where and how to invest their design and development resources.
