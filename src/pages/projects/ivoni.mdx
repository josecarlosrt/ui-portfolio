# Ivoni Beauty: From Logo to Full Brand System & Shopify Build

## Context
Ivoni Beauty came to me with a simple starting request: a logo. What
followed was a full-stack branding engagement, I ended up owning the
identity, brand voice, and design system end to end, and I'm now building
the Shopify site that carries that system into production. For a modern
beauty brand entering a crowded, highly visual category, the identity
needed to do more than look good in isolation, it needed to hold up
across product photography, packaging language, and a full e-commerce
experience.

## Goals
- **Cohesive Brand Identity:** Build outward from the logo into a complete
  visual language, color, typography, voice that reads as "warm
  sophistication": editorial contrast paired with soft, accessible luxury.
- **Editorial Typography System:** Pair a serif display face with a clean
  sans-serif interface font to balance emotional brand moments with
  functional e-commerce UI.
- **Shopify-Ready Component Library:** Design buttons, form fields, badges,
  and product cards as reusable, stated components (default, hover,
  disabled) so the system translates directly into Shopify's theme
  architecture during development.

## Process

### 1. Building the Identity Outward From the Logo
With only a logo as the starting brief, I defined Ivoni's full brand
foundation: a burgundy primary paired with blush pink and rose gold
accents, set against warm cream surfaces instead of stark white — a
deliberate choice to make the palette feel closer to a beauty counter
than a generic e-commerce template. I extended this into brand voice
work, shaping how Ivoni "speaks" across the site and product copy.

![Ivoni Beauty color system and typography pairing](/images/projects/ivoni-3.webp)

### 2. An Editorial Type System
I paired Playfair Display for headlines with Montserrat for interface
text — a classic editorial pairing that lets emotional moments (like
"Belleza para ti") feel elevated, while keeping product data, labels,
and UI copy clean and legible at small sizes.

### 3. A Shopify-Ready Component Library
Knowing development would follow, I designed every component with
Shopify's theme structure in mind: primary, blush, secondary, and ghost
button states; stock-status badges (Nuevo, En Stock, Últimas Piezas,
Agotado); and a full product card with image, title, variant, price, and
add-to-cart action, matching the layout Shopify's product grid expects.

## Outcome
What started as a logo request grew into full ownership of Ivoni Beauty's
brand identity and design system — color, typography, voice, and a
component library ready for e-commerce. I'm currently building the
Shopify site myself, using this same token system to keep the storefront
visually consistent with the brand from day one.
