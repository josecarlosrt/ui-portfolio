# Vitality PT: A Scalable Webflow Experience

## Context
Vitality PT required a digital presence that accurately reflected their core values: professionalism, trust, and patient-first care. The challenge was to move beyond a generic medical template and create a modern, high-converting landing experience. The client needed a platform that was not only visually compelling but also easy to scale and maintain as their practice grows.

## Goals
- **Design System Creation:** Establish a scalable, accessible visual identity in Figma, utilizing a minimalist aesthetic to highlight critical user pathways without overwhelming the visitor.
- **Scalable Architecture:** Translate the high-fidelity designs into a pixel-perfect Webflow build using structured, maintainable class naming conventions.
- **Performance & Conversion:** Ensure the site is optimized for Core Web Vitals and technical SEO, creating a seamless, fast-loading booking experience across all devices.

## Process
The project was divided into two distinct but deeply connected phases:

### 1. Design System (Figma)
I started by establishing a clean design system in Figma. Recognizing that medical websites often suffer from visual clutter, I leaned into a minimalist layout with intentional negative space and a soothing color palette. I built a reusable component library (buttons, typography scales, card layouts) to ensure consistency. The user flow was mapped to drive visitors naturally toward the primary call-to-action: scheduling a consultation.

### 2. Development (Webflow)
With the design system locked in, I moved into Webflow. I prioritized a clean backend structure, avoiding messy, duplicated classes. By using a systematic approach to class naming and variables, the foundation of the site is robust and ready for future updates. Furthermore, I applied semantic HTML and technical SEO best practices, such as proper heading hierarchies and optimized assets, to ensure the site performs exceptionally well on search engines and is ready for generative engine optimization (GEO).

## Outcome
The result is a fully responsive, lightweight, and accessible website that empowers Vitality PT to connect with their patients effectively. The structured Webflow build means the client now has a reliable digital asset that can grow alongside their business, while the meticulous design system ensures brand consistency across every breakpoint.
