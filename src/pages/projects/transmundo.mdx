# Transmundo: Wordpress Redesign

## Discovery & Research
Transmundo, a travel agency based in Honduras, required a website redesign to modernize the user experience, prepare for an upcoming launch, and ultimately improve conversion rates. While the agency provided creative freedom, the core challenge was translating minimal, concise inputs into a cohesive platform. A key functional requirement was to integrate an interactive and easy-to-use quick form powered by AI , which required a strategic UX approach to reduce friction in the travel planning process.

## Findings
The original site suffered from an inverted visual hierarchy and high cognitive load. The primary user goal (finding travel solutions) was overshadowed by internal company directories, resulting in a confusing user journey, dead ends, and a rigid layout that alienated mobile users.

## Visual Direction
The client prefers a clean and modern site that combines elements such as boxes with rounded edges, blur effects, and subtle animations. They really liked the round boxes and blur usage on Avianca’s site(avianca.com). Also, inspiration was drawn from a Dribble shot(dribbble), with a specific request for a homepage hero image that utilizes a card-style presentation to immediately grab the user's attention.

## Other Visual Direction
The design must prominently feature their Instagram page, making it a central element of the user experience. The brand's color scheme, specifically using "blocks of blue," must be integrated consistently across other pages.

## UX Strategy
Our UX strategy prioritizes a seamless, intuitive path from discovery to booking, ensuring that the visual aesthetic directly supports usability. The information architecture was engineered to funnel users efficiently, relying on a modern, minimalist design with an intentional use of white space to showcase brand colors and convey confidence.

## Core Pillars
1. **Conversion-Centric Flow:** The primary goal of the structural redesign is to remove usability barriers and improve conversion rates.
2. **Performance as UX:** We placed a high priority on Core Web Vitals metrics, recognizing that fast load times and technical stability are fundamental to a positive modern user experience.
3. **Search Engine Synergy:** The wireframes and underlying structure were designed with search engine optimization (AEO) and structured technical SEO (including schema and sitemaps) in mind to capture high-intent search traffic.
